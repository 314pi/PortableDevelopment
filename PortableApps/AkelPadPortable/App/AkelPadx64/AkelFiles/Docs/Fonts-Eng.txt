*** Description ***

Dynamical fonts loading without installation in system.


*** Functions ***

Fonts::Main
Main function for start and stop plugin.


*** Installation ***

1. Copy "Plugs\*.*" to "{AkelPad}\AkelFiles\Plugs".
2. Put fonts files in directory "{AkelPad}\AkelFiles\Plugs\Fonts".
