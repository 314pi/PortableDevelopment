*** Description ***

Work with clipboard.


*** Functions ***

Clipboard::Capture
Capture clipboard.

Clipboard::PasteSerial
Insert serial number.

Clipboard::SelAutoCopy
Automatic copy selection.

Clipboard::Paste
Paste text that was copied outside the SelAutoCopy.

Clipboard::Settings
Settings dialog.


*** Clipboard::PasteSerial ***

Hotkeys (global):
Ctrl+F9   - insert, skipping delimiters.
Ctrl+F10  - insert, replacing delimiter with tabulation.
Ctrl+F11  - insert as is.

Usage:
1. Start plugin.
2. Open document from which you want to copy serial.
3. Switch keyboard layout, usually English (En).
4. Copy serial number to clipboard, e.g. "1111-2222-3333-4444".
5. Open window in which you want to paste serial.
6. Activate first serial number field.
7. Switch keyboard layout, usually English (En).
8. Press hotkey, e.g. Ctrl+F9.
